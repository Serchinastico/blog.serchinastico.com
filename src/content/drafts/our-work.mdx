# App #1

Our first client came with a working mobile app, but they pivoted so much with the feedback they received from it, they ditched it and wanted to build a new one. We started off by creating a non-functional prototype with [React Native](https://reactnative.dev/) and [Expo](https://expo.dev/). The app used [fake data](https://github.com/faker-js/faker) to fill in the screens, and it simulated API calls to show loading states and errors. This first prototype gave us a lot of information to take on the next step of the project, it allowed us to test the usability patterns that better suited the app, it gave us clarity to build the Backend/API and helped us prioritize features.
